About Us

Founded in April of 2018, founders Andrew, Stephan, and Ben decided to create a api to collate NYC Public transport information out of personal need and lack of availablity. This api (Named Bus It!) combines realtime information of NYC MTA bus locations and weather, as well as weather alerts accross the transit system.
